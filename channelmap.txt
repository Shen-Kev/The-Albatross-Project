channel 1: throttle : pin 2
channel 2: aileron : pin 3
channel 3: elevator : pin 4
channel 4: rudder : pin 5
channel 5: flight mode : pin 6
channel 6: other flight mode : pin 7
maybe even have more pwm channels for other sensors?

servo2Pin: L aileron : 15
servo3Pin R aileron : 18
servo4Pin elevator : 19
servo5Pin rudder : 22
servo6Pin : other pwm actuator: 23
servo7Pin : other pwm actuator 29
